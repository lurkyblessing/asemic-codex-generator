What is this?

This is the Asemic Codex Generator, a tool designed to turn language into an unreadable, illuminated artifact. It was built as a weaponized obsession with texts like the Codex Seraphinianus and the Voynich Manuscript.

By procedurally generating an alien writing system—complete with a fictive grammar, ligatures, and a variable-width calligraphic brush engine—it translates your poems into an untranslatable, color-coded script.

Created by fuckshitpoet, this project exists for poets who want to write real poems and watch them become beautifully obscured, aged relics. It is a space where sadness and whimsy collide into something entirely new, hidden in plain sight.

Written in no known hand. Rendered entirely in your browser.
